Fun Facts

The “Golden Rule” is the rule of ~1oz. of liquid in ~17-23 seconds for an espresso extraction. The ideal coffee brewing temperature is between 195°F (90.6°C) and 205°F (96.1°C). Coffee is the second most traded commodity in the world, after oil. Gelato has a higher proportion of milk and a lower proportion of cream and eggs in contrast to a conventional Ice Cream.
